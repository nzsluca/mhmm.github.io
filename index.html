VIDEOs

In the early days we started off with mood and party remembrance videos, over the years our profile has expanded to include one shorter and one longer form interview series, as well as round-table discussions, and other fun projects.
YOUTUBE CHANEL

Launched as a part of our Házi event series focusing on all-Hungarian lineups, this lovely little interview series has grown its own legs. We have evolved the concept to include not just musicians, but important under-represented members of the entertainment industry scene we are a part of. Eventually these videos will form a longer form film about Budapest’s underground music scene.
